# 🔒 PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated:** June 2026

### 1. Introduction
**Mature Bot** ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot and web dashboard.

### 2. Information We Collect
**2.1 Automatically Collected Data:**
* **Discord User ID & Username:** To identify users, manage permissions, and display profiles.
* **Discord Guild (Server) ID:** To manage server-specific settings and backups.
* **Server Permissions:** To verify administrative access and enforce security rules.

**2.2 Economy & Game Data:**
* Virtual currency balance, inventory items (animals, collectibles), and transaction history.

**2.3 Premium Subscription Data:**
* Subscription tier, start/expiration dates, and premium activation codes used.

**2.4 Server Configuration:**
* Custom bot settings, enabled/disabled features, and backup data (server structure, roles, channels).

**2.5 Dashboard Usage:**
* IP address (for security and rate limiting), browser type, and session cookies.

### 3. How We Use Your Information
We use collected data for the following purposes:
* **Bot Functionality:** Processing commands, managing the economy, and enforcing antinuke rules.
* **Premium Features:** Verifying subscription status and granting tier-specific perks.
* **Security & Moderation:** Tracking command logs and user actions to prevent abuse.
* **Customer Support:** Resolving issues related to your account or premium purchases.

### 4. Data Storage & Security
* **Storage Location:** Data is stored in secure databases hosted on **Render.com** cloud infrastructure.
* **Encryption:** Data transmitted between your device, Discord, and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
* **Access Controls:** Only authorized developers can access production databases.
* **Data Retention:** Active user data is retained while you use the Bot. Inactive economy data may be purged after 12 months of inactivity. Backup data is stored for 30 days before automatic deletion.

### 5. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services to operate the Bot:
* **Discord API:** For bot functionality and user authentication.
* **Render.com:** For hosting and database management.
* **UPI Payment Gateway:** For processing premium subscription payments.

These third parties have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect your information.

### 6. Data Sharing
**We DO NOT:**
* Sell your personal data to third parties.
* Share your data for marketing purposes.

**We MAY share data:**
* With Discord, Inc. as required for bot functionality.
* With payment processors to complete UPI transactions.
* With law enforcement if strictly required by law.

### 7. Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your data:
* **Access:** Request a copy of your data by contacting support.
* **Deletion:** Request deletion of your economy and profile data by contacting support or using the `!deletedata` command (if available).
* **Opt-Out:** Remove the bot from your server to stop all data collection immediately.

### 8. Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

### 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
